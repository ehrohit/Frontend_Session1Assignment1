Scrambled eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite food.Here is a recepie for deleciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 sceonds ( as if you are making and omlete).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
